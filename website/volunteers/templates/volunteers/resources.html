Submit reimbursements

Please click here to submit your receipt for reimbursement. Please note reimbursements are done on a monthly basis and should be submitted before the 20th. You should expect to receive the reimbursement within 2 days, not automatically. Full grocery reimbursements are available while grant funding exists to cover the costs. You will be notified when we are approaching our funding limit and revert to a $200/month per chef reimbursement.

Submit recipes

Please click here to submit your recipe to our community cookbook. The People’s Pantry Community Cookbook will be a collection of recipes from our chefs and bakers in addition to a discussion about how food injustices and food insecurities have always existed yet became highlighted due to COVID-19 and how The People’s Pantry and mutual aid organizations from all over came to be.
